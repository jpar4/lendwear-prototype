🎨 UI/UX Concept

LendWear is designed with a **User-Centric & Data-Driven** approach, ensuring that business casual fashion is accessible and personalized for every professional. Our interface focuses on a seamless transition from onboarding to weekly management.

Key Screens & Flow

1.  **Personalized Onboarding**: Users begin by inputting their physical measurements and selecting their gender. This data forms the foundation of our fitting algorithm.
2.  **Style Preference Quiz**: To understand the user's "Office Vibe," we conduct a visual quiz. Whether it's *Conservative Corporate* or *Creative Professional*, the app learns to match the user's aesthetic.
3.  **Tiered Subscription Model**: We offer two distinct tiers to cater to different professional needs:
    * **Essential ($40/wk)**: High-street premium brands for daily reliability.
    * **Executive ($80/wk)**: Luxury designer labels with unlimited swaps and premium services.
4.  **Smart Weekly Dashboard**: The core experience where users view their "Outfit of the Week," curated based on real-time weather data (e.g., Auckland's variable climate) and personal style history.

Design Principles
* **Minimalism**: A clean, monochromatic slate/navy theme to reflect professional standards.
* **Interactivity**: Integrated 'Swap' features to give users control over their weekly rotation.
* **Personalization**: Real-time feedback loops through rating systems to improve future recommendations.
